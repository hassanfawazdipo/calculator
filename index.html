100,000 Registered Companies

Once we reach 100,000 registered companies, each verified business will receive a unique digital certificate—boosting brand credibility, user trust, and market reputation.

Business Legitimacy Support

We’ll assist registered businesses in securing official CAC (Corporate Affairs Commission) certification—empowering entrepreneurs with the legal recognition they need to scale confidently.

500,000 Companies Milestone

At 500,000 registered companies, we’ll launch a TV series called "Day in a Life"—a live storytelling show across TikTok, YouTube, and more—giving users the spotlight to showcase their brand, journey, and services globally.

1 Million Companies & Beyond

Upon hitting 1 million companies, we’ll introduce a robust Learning Hub—providing skill development courses, mentorship, and entrepreneurship guidance. This powerful move helps reduce unemployment and uplifts Nigeria’s workforce.
